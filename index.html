Bonus

Hold bonus

The smart contract calculates the hold bonus based on the date of the last withdrawal of funds or the date of the deposit if the funds have not been withdrawn yet. If you did not request withdrawal, you will receive an additional 0.1% / day.

Fund bonus

The smart contract adds an additional 0.1% / day up to your earnings for every 1,000,000 TRX total invested.

Referral bonus

The smart contract adds 0.1% / day for each 1,000,000 TRX deposited in total by your referrals of all tiers.
